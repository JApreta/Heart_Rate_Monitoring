Did you know over 48% of Americans have heart disease? According to the American Medical Association, there are over 121.5 adults living in the United States with some form of cardiovascular illness. While this may seem alarming, one of the best steps you can take towards a positive prognosis is keeping in touch with your heart. That's were our heart rate monitor steps in, using an easy to navigate online portal, any patient with one of our devices can keep track of their heart rate and SpO2 data. Physicians can also utilize the portal to keep track of all their patients, and with https security and advanced account protections, both patients and physicians can have peace of mind that their data is protected.
